Memorandum of understanding

We are excited to announce that Vosa Tech, Tirunelveli, has officially signed an MOU with EDII-TN to collaboratively organize a Private School Hackathon across six districts—Madurai, Thoothukudi, Tirunelveli, Virudhunagar, Kanyakumari, and Tenkasi! This initiative aims to empower school students with hands-on experience in innovation, problem-solving, and technology, fostering the next generation of tech leaders. A special thanks to Dr. Ambalavanan IAAS for signing this MOU and supporting this transformative journey. We are proud to be part of this mission to inspire young minds and drive digital innovation!
